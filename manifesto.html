The Human Core Manifesto

Established in 2026 to protect the unique value of biological creativity.

01. Purpose

Human Core exists to bridge the transparency gap between human and synthetic creation. In an era of infinite generated content, knowing the origin of a spark is a fundamental right for the audience and a badge of honor for the creator.

02. Ownership & Training

By using the Human Core badge, the author declares that their work is a result of intentional biological effort. This badge acts as an explicit non-consent for the use of the work in AI training datasets without specific human authorization and fair economic compensation.

03. Verification Ethics

The standard is voluntary. Its power lies in the honesty of the community. Falsifying a Human Core certification undermines the trust of the creative ecosystem and the value of human labor.

Official Classification

  • HC-Pure: Human conception and execution. Zero generative AI tools used in the process.
  • HC-Assist: Human core. AI limited to grammar, audio cleaning, or technical resolution scaling.
  • HC-Hybrid: Creative direction by human. Execution involves generative output from AI models.

Signed by the Human Core Community, 2026.
